GIAN THE CHOW CHOW


LOOKS

  • Can have either a short,smooth,glossy, and soft coat or a flat/curly long coat
  • A very very small dog

PERSONALITY

  • Loud happy dogs that thrive from human company
  • An active dog

SOPHIE THE CHIHUAHUA


LOOKS

  • Has a short soft coat
  • Can have multiple combinations of the colors black,white tan, red
  • May have markings on their coats

PERSONALITY

  • Have a fun and Jolly Temperament
  • They love to play, making them great companions for children
  • They tend to bark a lot
  • Don't like being left alone
